Background


We all think off music in form of audio.
Pintone presents the Music as an illustration.
It maps the characteristics of a composition with illustrations
by deciphering computerized or electronic signs.
The first similar tool was created in 1976 and with advancement of
technology Pintone presents many new ways of abstract visualization.

Overview

There are various music and frequency visualizers. Pintone slightly differs from them. It divides one press of the key into time-intervals. One-time interval will be represented as a circle and a rectangular segment. The size of the said circle grows according to the time the key was pressed for. The rectangular segments will have fixed width and the quantity will vary based on time. The final image is a combination of circles and segments, creating a beautiful, colorful abstract masterpiece.
